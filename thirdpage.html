    2021-2022 Season Record

    FTHS Soccer 2021-2022 Season Stats

    The FTHS Soccer team started the season 2-0-1, with two 5-0 wins against rival Manalapan and Jackson Memorial. The team then tied Red Bank Catholic 1-1. However, the team finished there stellar regular season 11-0. They had huge wins during the stretch, 8-0 over Middletown North, 9-0 over Long Branch, 7-0 over Middletown south, and 6-0 against Shore. However, the teams season did not end, as they went into the Shore Conference Tournamemt. In the second round they defeated Point Pleasant Boro 5-0, defeated Middletown South in the SemiFinals, and was victorious in the finals, 3-1, defeating Toms River North. They then entered the NJSIAA Tournament, crushing West Windsor-Plainsboro South 9-0 in round one. They then defeated Jackson Memorial and Manalapan, and won 4-3 in the finals over Rancocas Valley. Two games later, they were defeated 1-0 against Westfield, but their season was one to remember.

    Team History

    The FTHS Soccer Team went 22-1-1 in the 2021-2022 season, 13-1-1 in the 2020-2021 season, 14-6-3 in the 2019-2020 season, 14-5-1 during the 2018-2019 season, and 23-1-1 during the 2017-2018 season.
